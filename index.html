Circa 2018

World's First Blindfolded Suryanamaskar

Himanshu devised a globally first-of-its-kind Blindfolded Suryanamaskar — the slowest form of Suryanamaskar integrating Pranayama, Chakra Meditation on Mantras, and the elemental essence of Panchmahabhoota. Suitable for ages 8 to 80, this vinyasa-style practice elevates self-awareness and inner balance.

Ongoing

Chair Suryanamaskar — Inspired by My Mother

Inspired by his late mother, Himanshu conceptualized a first-of-its-kind Chair Suryanamaskar — an innovation to help senior citizens and the elderly experience the full benefits of Suryanamaskar while seated, with dignity and ease. A tribute born from love.

Juhu Beach Beautification & Yoga Park

As a social activist, Dr. Mehta has worked extensively on the beautification of Juhu Beach — transforming neglected areas into serene, functional Yoga zones, gardens, and a dedicated Yoga Park. For over 11 years, he has conducted free Saturday sessions as Karseva, making Yoga accessible to all.
