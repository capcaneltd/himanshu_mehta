A Heartfelt & Transformational Journey with Pratima Tripathi

What began as a professional banking relationship with Pratima Tripathi evolved into a deep journey of trust, creativity, and emotional bonding that became an integral part of my life and work.

Pratima joined my yoga group and became one of its most vibrant members, bringing a unique blend of creativity and warmth. Her meticulous involvement, especially in refining photographs and conceptualizing the unique miniature format of my book, added a distinctive global appeal.

Beyond work, her presence added life to our community. Her bond with my family, particularly my mother, was deeply touching. A sacred and unforgettable moment remains January 1st, 2025 — when despite being critically unwell, my mother blessed Pratima during her birthday celebration, just hours before peacefully departing.

For nearly three years, her dedication to yoga brought about a remarkable personal transformation, moving from frequent illnesses to complete wellness.

The greatest insight: true success is built not just on individual effort, but on meaningful relationships and shared vision.
