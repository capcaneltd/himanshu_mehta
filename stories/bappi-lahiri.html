A Memorable Meeting with Bappi Lahiri – Insights Beyond Music

Meeting with Bappi Lahiri, the legendary music composer and director of Bollywood, was not just a moment of pride — it was an experience filled with warmth, connection, and meaningful exchange.

I was fortunate to be invited to celebrate his birthday, where I had a brief yet impactful rendezvous with him. During our interaction, I shared insights about yoga, particularly the importance of starting with Pranayama. His response was genuinely enthusiastic — he expressed a deep curiosity about yoga and a desire to begin his journey.

A few days later, I had another opportunity at a film premiere, where he was the chief guest. This second interaction turned into a friendly and heartfelt conversation, strengthening the connection built earlier.

Though he is no longer with us, the insights from Bappi Lahiri and the warmth of our meetings remain unforgettable, reinforcing my journey of sharing yoga with influential personalities.

The key takeaway: true greatness carries humility and a willingness to learn.
