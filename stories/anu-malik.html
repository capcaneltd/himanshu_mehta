Meeting with Anu Malik – A Musical Legend’s Appreciation for Yoga & Knowledge

Meeting with Anu Malik, one of India’s most celebrated music composers and singers, was both an honour and a deeply fulfilling experience.

This memorable interaction took place during a marathon organized by the Swami Vivekanand Connect Group, where passion for fitness, wellness, and community spirit came together.

During the meeting, I had the privilege of presenting my book to him. As he thoughtfully went through its pages, his genuine curiosity and appreciation were clearly visible. What made this interaction truly special were his reactions to certain unique and lesser-known topics in yoga — subjects he admitted he had never come across before.

This meeting was not just about presenting a book; it was about bridging the worlds of music, mindfulness, and meaningful living.
