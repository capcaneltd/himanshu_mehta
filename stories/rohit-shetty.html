A Remarkable Meeting with Rohit Shetty – From Cinema to Conscious Living

Meeting with Rohit Shetty, one of India’s most successful film directors, was an experience that beautifully blended the worlds of cinema and wellness.

This interaction took place during the inauguration of a police station, an initiative Rohit Shetty generously supported. I had the privilege of presenting my book "Making Of A Yogi" to him.

He received it with genuine curiosity and appreciation, noting that he had never seen such a unique perspective on yoga before. His openness to learning beyond his professional domain was truly inspiring.

The key takeaway: true achievers remain lifelong learners, always open to new dimensions of growth.
