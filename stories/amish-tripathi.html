An Inspiring Meeting with Amish Tripathi – Insights on Wisdom, Writing & Purpose

A meeting with Amish Tripathi, one of India’s most celebrated authors known for redefining mythology through modern storytelling, was truly an honour and intellectually enriching experience.

This experience took place during the Healing Our Earth program, where he was invited as a distinguished guest. Thanks to Nilkumar and the organizing team, I had the rare privilege of a one-on-one online interaction with him for nearly 15 minutes. Despite his global commitments and frequent residence in London, Amish ji was warm, grounded, and deeply engaging in conversation.

Our discussion revolved around philosophy, cultural roots, and the deeper connection between ancient wisdom and modern life. What stood out most were the insights on how timeless values, when presented with clarity and conviction, can inspire millions across generations. His perspective beautifully aligned with the essence of yoga — bridging tradition with contemporary relevance.

The key takeaway: when knowledge is shared with purpose, it becomes a powerful force for transformation.
