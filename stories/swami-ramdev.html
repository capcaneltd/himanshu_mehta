A Memorable Meeting with Swami Ramdev – A Landmark Moment for My Book

My meeting with Swami Ramdev became one of the most defining milestones in my journey as a yoga practitioner and author. A globally recognized yoga guru, Swamiji has transformed millions of lives through his teachings, television programs reaching over 30 million viewers, and large-scale yoga movements across India and internationally.

The journey began when I shared my proof copy with Col. Dheer, a respected war veteran and long-time associate of Swamiji at Patanjali Yogpeeth. After carefully reviewing my book for over 10 days, he was deeply impressed and strongly recommended it for an official launch.

This led to an unforgettable moment — on 8th February, I was invited to Patanjali, where my book was launched live on Aastha TV at 7 AM, in the presence of more than 1,000 students.

This experience with Swami Ramdev was truly heartwarming. He welcomed me with a warm embrace, publicly felicitated me, and appreciated my efforts to inspire people through yoga. His blessing, in the form of a sacred mala, remains one of my most cherished possessions.

The greatest insights from Swami Ramdev were simple yet profound — when your intention is to serve humanity through yoga, recognition follows naturally.
