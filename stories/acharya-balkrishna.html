A Purposeful Meeting with Acharya Balkrishna – Where Yoga Meets Ayurveda

A meeting with Acharya Balkrishna, the visionary co-founder of Patanjali Ayurved and a globally respected authority on Ayurveda and natural healing, was both a privilege and an intellectually enriching experience.

This experience took place at Patanjali Yogpeeth during my visit for the launch of my book.

During our interaction, I had the honour of presenting my book to him. His genuine curiosity and deep engagement with the content — especially topics that connected yoga with ancient Indian wellness principles — made the exchange truly meaningful.

This meeting strengthened my resolve to continue bridging authentic yogic wisdom with the broader wellness ecosystem.

The key insights revolve around the seamless unity of yoga and Ayurveda — two pillars of India’s ancient wellness heritage.
