Meeting with Bikram Choudhury – Mastery Under Extreme Conditions

My encounter with Bikram Choudhury, the globally renowned founder of Hot Yoga, was an unforgettable experience.

During his visit to India, I had the rare privilege of a one-on-one interaction for over 45 minutes. Practicing 26 postures in 90 minutes at 41°C was not just physically intense — it was mentally elevating.

His studio was a vibrant hub, where I practiced alongside Bollywood personalities like Anil Kapoor, Sonam Kapoor, Manisha Koirala, Sonu Sood, and others.

Beyond the glamour, the real insights were about mastering the body under extreme conditions, cultivating discipline, and maintaining consistency.

While his journey later encountered challenges, the wisdom I gained remains timeless.
