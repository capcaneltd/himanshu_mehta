Meeting with Bikram Choudhury – Mastery Under Extreme Conditions

My encounter with Bikram Choudhury, the globally renowned founder of Hot Yoga, was an unforgettable experience that pushed my endurance, discipline, and inner focus to extraordinary levels.

Read More

During his visit to India, I had the rare privilege of a one-on-one interaction for over 45 minutes, with treasured photographs capturing that moment. The purpose of this meeting was to deepen my understanding of Hot Yoga alongside my Iyengar practice.


Practicing 26 postures in 90 minutes at 41°C was not just physically intense — it was mentally elevating. This experience with Bikram Choudhury pushed my endurance, discipline, and inner focus to extraordinary levels.


His studio was a vibrant hub, where I practiced alongside renowned Bollywood personalities like Anil Kapoor, Sonam Kapoor, Manisha Koirala, Sonu Sood, Rakhi Sawant, Raima Sen, Riya Sen, and Gul Panag — all of whom acknowledged and respected my advanced level of practice.


Beyond the glamour, the real insights from Bikram Choudhury were about mastering the body under extreme conditions, cultivating discipline, and maintaining consistency. I also developed close connections with his inner circle — his niece Paloma was inspired by my journey and transformed her life through yoga, and I had the opportunity to practice with his wife, Rajashree Choudhury.


While his journey later encountered challenges, the wisdom I gained remains timeless. This experience not only expanded my yogic horizons but also strengthened my authority as a deeply committed and versatile practitioner of yoga.

A Transformative Meeting with Dr. Hansa Yogendra – My Foundation in Classical Yoga

My meeting with Dr. Hansa Yogendra at The Yoga Institute, Santacruz, stands as one of the most grounding and enriching chapters of my yoga journey — a relationship spanning over 15 years.

Read More

While my initiation into yoga began in 1978, it was from 1981 onwards — when I formally joined this prestigious, over 125-year-old institute — that my path truly took shape. I was deeply fortunate to receive the blessings and direct guidance of Shri Yogendra, followed by his son Dr. Jayadeva Yogendra, and later under the inspiring leadership of Dr. Hansa Yogendra.


Over nearly 15 years, I returned to the institute multiple times, immersing myself in their profound teachings — especially in pranayama and yogic philosophy.


Years later, when I authored my book, I had the honor of presenting it to Dr. Hansa Yogendra. She was pleasantly surprised by the depth of research and authenticity of my work, and her heartfelt appreciation and blessings remain one of my greatest validations.


Her words still resonate deeply: she described me as a charismatic personality dedicated to spreading yoga and acknowledged the sincerity and effort behind my work. The greatest insights from Dr. Hansa Yogendra were rooted in simplicity, discipline, and self-transformation — yoga not just as practice, but as a way of life.

A Transformative Meeting with Sadhvi Bhagwati Saraswati – At the International Yoga Festival

My meeting with Sadhvi Bhagwati Saraswati at the International Yoga Festival was a deeply enriching and life-elevating experience — connecting me to a worldwide family of yoga.

Read More

Hosted at Parmarth Niketan Ashram, this globally renowned festival — her visionary creation — brings together over 2,000 participants from more than 110 countries every year. A Stanford graduate who renounced a conventional life in the West to embrace spirituality in India, Sadhviji's journey itself is profoundly inspiring.


I had the privilege of attending two week-long festivals, beginning with my first visit in March 2015, where I also experienced the joy of Holi celebrations with thousands of participants. It was a vibrant blend of devotion and celebration — music, colors, dance, and pure bliss on the banks of Maa Ganga.


From early morning Kundalini sadhana at 4 a.m. to evening Ganga Aarti, kirtans, and cultural programs, every moment was immersive and spiritually uplifting.


The greatest insights from Sadhvi Bhagwati Saraswati were about unity, surrender, and higher purpose — yoga not just as practice, but as a path to global harmony. This experience expanded my vision, connecting me to a worldwide family of yoga committed to collective growth and consciousness.
